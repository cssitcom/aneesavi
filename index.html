About Me

Licensed Marriage & Family Therapist with over 10 years of experience in holistic healing approaches.

My journey into therapy began with my own healing process and the profound realization that we all have an innate capacity for growth and transformation. I integrate traditional therapeutic techniques with mindfulness-based practices to create a safe, nurturing space for exploration and healing.

I hold a Master's degree in Counseling Psychology from the University of California, Santa Cruz and I am licensed by the California Board of Behavioral Sciences. My approach is rooted in compassion, authenticity, and the belief that every individual's story is sacred.

My Therapeutic Approach

Holistic & Integrative

I believe in treating the whole person - mind, body, and spirit. My approach integrates various evidence-based therapeutic modalities with mindfulness and somatic awareness to support your complete well-being.

Therapeutic Modalities

CBT

Helping couples and individuals understand and transform their emotional responses.

Mindfulness-Based Therapy

Incorporating present-moment awareness to reduce anxiety and increase self-compassion.

Somatic Approaches

Recognizing the wisdom of the body in healing and processing experiences.

Narrative Therapy

Exploring and reauthoring the stories that shape your identity and relationships.
